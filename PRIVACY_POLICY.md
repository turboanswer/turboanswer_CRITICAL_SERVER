# Privacy Policy for Turbo Answer

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Turbo Answer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Username, email address, and password when you create an account
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely through Stripe for Pro subscriptions
- **Chat Messages**: Conversations with our AI assistant for providing responses

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Data**: App interaction patterns, feature usage, session duration
- **Voice Data**: Temporary voice recordings for speech-to-text processing (immediately deleted after processing)

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- Provide AI-powered responses through Google Gemini integration
- Process voice commands for hands-free interaction
- Manage your subscription and billing through Stripe
- Maintain conversation history within your account

### Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
- Develop new features based on user needs
- Ensure technical functionality and security

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Services
- **Google Gemini AI**: Your messages are processed by Google's AI service to generate responses
- **Stripe**: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions
- **Voice Processing**: Speech-to-text conversion (processed locally when possible)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process.

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
- Secure authentication and session management
- Payment data processed securely through Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)
- Voice recordings deleted immediately after processing

### Data Retention
- Account information: Retained while account is active
- Chat history: Stored until you delete conversations
- Usage analytics: Aggregated data retained for service improvement

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control
- View and export your chat history
- Delete individual conversations or entire account
- Update account information and preferences
- Cancel subscription at any time

### Voice Data Rights
- Voice recordings are processed in real-time and not stored
- You can disable voice features in app settings
- No voice data is shared with third parties beyond processing needs

## Subscription and Billing

- Subscription data used only for service provisioning
